Real Applications through Simulations & Case Studies

The Only way to Master Finance is to actually do Finance

At ISFB you don't just study Financial Models - you actually build them. Real financiers use judgement and instinct that you will hone through simulated M&A deals, equity research assignments, portfolio strategy exercises and more.

Our world class simulations enable you to apply your knowledge to complex business problems of Valuations and forecasting, crisis responses and regulatory navigation that mirror the real world and go beyond the textbook.

    Apply through
    Harvard-style business case simulations
    • Valuation of early-stage startups.
    • Capital budgeting for manufacturing expansion.
    • Turnaround strategy for a distressed financial services firm.
    Industry-led Fintech Competitions
    • Building a digital lending model for underserved MSMEs.
    • Structuring compliance workflows for a payments startup.
    • Using AI to underwrite credit risk in tier 2/3 cities of India.
    Deal-Maker competitions
    • Structuring a private equity deal with exit strategy.
    • Valuation of a Series B startup in the D2C space.
    • Negotiating a buyout between two competing firms.
    Peer debates
    • Monetary Policy vs Fiscal Intervention in recession cycles.
    • ESG investing: Value creation or greenwashing?
    • Should India adopt a central bank digital currency (CBDC)?
    DCF models
    • Terminal value debates: Perpetuity vs exit multiple.
    • Sensitivity analysis and Monte Carlo scenarios.
    • Forecasting free cash flows for cyclical industries.
    Boardroom simulations
    • Budget approval and resource allocation for Q4.
    • Crisis simulation: Product recall, cyberattack, or regulatory crackdown.
    • M&A pitch to board and dissenting stakeholders.

    Graduate with a BBA Degree and a Global Finance Specialisation

    The Undergraduate in Finance and Business is a full time, on campus program built for flexibility and focus. Students are recommended to pursue a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) from NMIMS Centre for Distance and Online Education (CDOE) alongside their ISFB program.

    Students go on to specialise early through ACCA or CFA aligned pathways, building targeted expertise in investment banking, capital markets, global accounting, and asset management. This integrated approach delivers deep finance skills, real industry exposure, and a recognised BBA degree, with a global to grassroots experience.

    *ISFB neither issues these degrees nor is a partner to any university that issues them.
